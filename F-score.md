The F-score also called the F1 score is a measure of accuracy that is the harmonic mean of [[Precision]] and [[Recall]]. 
$$ F_1 = \frac{1}{\frac{\frac{1}{precision}+\frac{1}{recall}}{2}} = 2\frac{precision*recall}{precision+recall}
$$
F1 score gives a realistic measure of a test's performance in use cases like: measuring search, document classification and query classification performance.
